Welcome to David Anthony Hairdressing...

If you’re looking for a standard haircut with no frills and no special treatment then you’ve come to the wrong place.

This is because here at David Anthony Hairdressing we pride ourselves on treating every client as an individual, and as such we understand that every head of hair has unique requirements and everybody has definite ideas regarding the styling of their hair. We try hard not to do ‘standard’ and would much rather do ‘one of a kind that is unique to you’.

We never ride roughshod over your ideas, and welcome your views on what we do. Every stylist in the salon is just as experienced in the art of listening as they are in the art of hairdressing, and we encourage you to tell us what you want rather than us telling you what suits. Don’t worry if you don’t have a style in mind when you arrive though – we offer a full consultation service if required or some simple advice if not.
