Meet the team

  • David Director

    As founder and director of DAH, David, has worked in the industry for many years. David has a great passion for the hairdressing industry and a great passion for people.

    Because of this he has gained a wealth of knowledge about the industry and about the client’s needs and expectations. David created this fantastic brand new salon in the centre of Wilmslow and has brought together a highly successful team of hairdressers to not only produce amazing cutting, blowing and styling work, also providing every single client with excellent customers service in a professional comfortable environment for everyone.

  • Kate Senior Stylist

    As one of our senior stylists with over 20 years experience and having worked at David Anthony since 2009, she has grown an extensive clientele. Her vast knowledge of colour has given her a reputation as one of the top colour technicians, with awards to show for it. She continues to update her colour work and gain inspiration as a regular as a regular attendee at Wella Studios in Manchester.

    “I am so passionate about colour. I get so excited about new and trends that appear every season, and strive to achieve my own personal touch to insure every client, regardless of hair type or length so they leave knowing that they are bang on trend. From the most simple colour, to the most creative colour.”

  • Liz Senior Stylist

    Liz is one of our salon senior stylists with 12 years of hairdressing experience. Liz has been a member of the DA team for the last year and a half; she previously worked at Vidal Sassoon in Manchester. Liz is passionate about hair, all aspects of cutting, colouring and styling hair. Here she learnt new techniques of precision cutting and styling the hair to my clients needs.

    “Vidal Sassoon is one of my main influences which inspires me to create new looks everyday”

  • Hannah Senior Stylist

    Hannah joined the David Anthony team in 2009 in the original salon in Styal and has helped develop the salon to bring it into Wilmslow town centre. Quickly progressing to senior stylist, Hannah has a natural flair for both cutting and colour having a great understanding of all areas of colour techniques. Hannah has an extensive range of clients and is known as being one of the top colourists around. Along with her vast knowledge of colour and cutting techniques so she can tailor make it to any individuals needs.

    “I love it that no two days are the same and also love the excitement of meeting new client and building a relationship with each and every one of them.”

  • Jaye Stylist

    Jaye has been in the hairdressing industry for 2 ½ years and is fascinated with hairup. Jaye has partaken in a number of weddings and shows in her spare time she is also passionate attending courses and keeping up to date with current fashion forward styles and colour techniques.

    “I am passionate about pushing my own modern twist on hair using my enthusiasm and knowledge to tailor each individual client’s wishes and achieving great results.”

  • Becca Apprentice

    Becca has a brilliant understanding of current trends. She is insightful when it comes to fashion and the latest styles. She uses this knowledge to lead the way in her colour work. Becca is extremely popular with staff and clients likewise in her spare time with them regularly socialising.

    “Hairdressing is a huge part of my life and I love my job and look forward to pursuing my hairdressing career.”

  • Tash Apprentice

    As one of the youngest members of the ‘DAH’ team, she has continued to grow in ability and enthusiasm. She has worked hard in her College N.V.Q and has achieved numerous awards and accolades. She is a popular member of the DAH team and has been well received by the clients of the salon.

    “I’m looking forward to becoming a hair stylist to really get involved with all the action.”

  • Emily Saturday Girl

    I really enjoyed working with the team at David Anthony for the past year they’re a great crowd to work with, and everyone is really friendly.

  • Georgia

    Having started her hairdressing career 4 years ago, Georgia has become an accomplished colour technician who is very much in demand. She is now developing her skills, a creative cutter with a fresh modernistic approach.

    “I love making my client’s look and feel great; this makes my job feel really satisfying.”
