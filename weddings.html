Here you can view the many exceptional and distinctive hairstyles created by our fully qualified and highly experienced stylists over the last few years. This page is updated regularly so visit often if you’re still contemplating what to do with your hair next.

Of course we can’t guarantee you’ll get the exact same hairstyles as those seen in our gallery. This is because every head of hair has different characteristics that make it behave in a specific manner when cut, and it may not be possible to replicate a style perfectly. We also pride ourselves on adding small, yet unique, touches to every client’s style, so that the finished look is as individual as the person sporting it.

If you see a style you like then by all means mention this at your appointment, but don’t be surprised if your stylist makes minute changes so that your hair, when finished, is 133% unique to you. You might not be able to discern the differences but we will know they are there.

Hopefully after looking through the Gallery you’ll not only have a better understanding of what you yourself are looking for, you’ll also have a good idea of the type of hairstyles we can create. If none of the styles posted here suit your needs don’t panic…just come along with an idea of what you want and we will do the rest.

Enjoy browsing and we hope to see you in the salon soon.
